Privacy Policy for Blood Pressure Tracker

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Blood Pressure Tracker ("the App") handles information when you use it.

Blood Pressure Tracker is designed to be private by default. In version 1, the App works without account creation, cloud sync, advertising, or a backend service for storing your readings.

This policy applies to the Blood Pressure Tracker mobile application. It does not cover privacy practices of app stores, device manufacturers, mobile operating systems, or websites you may use to download or learn about the App.

Information We Collect

Health information you enter

When you use the App, you may choose to enter:

  • Blood pressure readings, including systolic and diastolic values
  • Pulse, if you decide to record it
  • Measurement date and time

This information is stored locally on your device so the App can show your history, trends, and PDF exports.

Information we do not ask for in the App

In version 1 of the App, we do not require:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Account registration
  • Password
  • Cloud profile

Analytics and advertising

This version of the App does not include third-party analytics SDKs or advertising SDKs.

How We Use Information

The App uses the information you enter only to provide its core features, including:

  • Saving your readings on your device
  • Showing your recent history and trends
  • Generating a PDF report when you request one
  • Displaying WHO-based blood pressure status guidance inside the App

The App is intended to support logging and review. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or emergency advice.

How Your Information Is Stored

Your readings are stored in a local database on your device.

Android automatic backup for the App is disabled in the current build configuration. This is intended to reduce unintended copying of your health data through platform backup systems.

Because your data is stored locally, access to your device may allow access to your readings. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device, including screen lock and device-level protections.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not automatically share your blood pressure readings with us or with third parties through a cloud sync service in version 1.

Information leaves the App only when you choose to export or share it yourself, such as when you:

  • Generate a PDF report
  • Share that PDF using your device's system share sheet

Once you share an exported file with another app, service, or person, their privacy practices apply to that copy.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your readings remain on your device until you edit them, delete them, clear the App's storage, or uninstall the App.

Because we do not maintain a user account database for version 1, there is no server-side account or cloud record for us to delete.

Internet and Offline Use

Core logging, history, trend review, and PDF generation are designed to work offline.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through a hosted account system because version 1 does not provide account registration or cloud storage.

If you believe a child has entered sensitive information into the App on a shared device, remove that information directly from the device or uninstall the App.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have privacy rights under applicable law. Because version 1 stores readings locally on your device rather than on our servers, many privacy controls are exercised directly by you inside the App or on your device, including:

  • Reviewing your saved readings
  • Editing or deleting readings
  • Deleting exported PDF files you created
  • Uninstalling the App or clearing its local storage

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
